Travel & Transportation

Maui's Kahului International Airport

1 Keolani Pl

Kahului, HI 96732

 (808)872-3830

The largest international airport on the island, the Kahului Airport has direct flights from the continental United States via Delta, United, American Airlines, Alaska Airlines, Hawaiian Airlines, and now Southwest Airlines

For travelers on a budget, it is also a possible option to book a less expensive flight with a layover in Honolulu.

From the Airport to Ka'anapali/Lahaina: You can take an Uber, taxi, rent a car, or see if the hotel you're staying at has airport shuttles.

The Road to Hana

This legendary road is packed with waterfalls, lava tubes, local eateries and hiking trails. When you think of a lush rainforest and cascading waterfalls - THIS IS IT! Be prepared for a long drive full of twists and turns, and give yourself plenty of time to stop and see everything!
